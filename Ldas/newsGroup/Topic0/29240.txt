McCutchen HR in 14th lifts Giants over Dodgers 7-5 (Apr 07, 2018) SAN FRANCISCO (AP)  Andrew McCutchen hit a three-run homer to cap a 12-pitch at-bat in the 14th inning, giving him a career-high six hits and the San Francisco Giants a 7-5 victory over the Los Angeles Dodgers on Saturday night.
