Clamping down: Wade, Heat stymie Cavaliers, 98-79 (Mar 27, 2018) MIAMI (AP)  Kelly Olynyk scored 19 points, Dwyane Wade blocked a pair of shots by LeBron James as part of a stifling defensive effort by Miami, and the Heat had little trouble on the way to beating the Cleveland Cavaliers 98-79 on Tuesday night.
