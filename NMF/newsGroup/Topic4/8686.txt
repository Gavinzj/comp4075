Jalen Brunson Beats Out Trae Young, Marvin Bagley III to Win 2018 Wooden Award Villanova Wildcats guard Jalen Brunson was named winner of the 2018 Wooden Award on Friday in Los Angeles.   Brunson beat out 14 other  finalists , including Arizona's Deandre Ayton, Duke's Marvin Bagley III and Oklahoma's Trae Young...
