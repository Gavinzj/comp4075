Bogaerts' slam, 6 RBIs carries Red Sox past Rays 10-3 (Apr 07, 2018) BOSTON (AP)  Xander Bogaerts hit the first grand slam by a Boston player since the end of the 2016 season and drove in a career-best six runs to carry the Red Sox to a 10-3 win over the Tampa Bay Rays on Saturday for their seventh straight victory.
