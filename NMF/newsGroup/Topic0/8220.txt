Inciarte drives in 4 runs as Braves pound Phillies 15-2 (Mar 31, 2018) ATLANTA (AP)  Ender Inciarte drove in four runs with three hits, Ryan Flaherty had four hits and scored four times, and the Atlanta Braves roughed up Vince Velasquez and the Philadelphia Phillies for a 15-2 win on Saturday night.
