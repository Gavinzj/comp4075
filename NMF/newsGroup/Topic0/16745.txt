Towns helps T-wolves top Grizzlies 113-94, closer to clinch (Apr 09, 2018) MINNEAPOLIS (AP)  Karl-Anthony Towns slammed his way to 24 points and grabbed 18 rebounds for the Minnesota Timberwolves, who toughed out a 113-94 victory over the Memphis Grizzlies on Monday night to move one step closer to a long-sought spot in the playoffs.
