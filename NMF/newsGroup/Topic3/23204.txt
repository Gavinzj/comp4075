WWE Wrestlemania 34: Ronda Rousey admits UFC defeats were 'worth it' after victorious wrestling debut Ronda Rousey claimed victory in a stunning WWE debut at Wrestlemania 34. Teaming up with veteran Kurt Angle in New Orleans, the 31-year-old defeated Stephanie McMahon and her partner Triple H in an encounter that thoroughly entertained the 78,133-strong crowd.
